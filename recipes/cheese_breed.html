Italian Herb Cheese Bread

A delicious buttery Italian cheese bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups warm water
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • ½ cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  1. Place warm water, flour, pepper Jack cheese, Italian seasoning, black pepper, Parmesan cheese, brown sugar, salt, and yeast in the pan of a bread machine in the order suggested by the manufacturer.
  2. Select White Bread or Basic cycle, then Start.
